Pre & Post Wax Care

A little prep and a little aftercare go a long way. Here's how to get the smoothest, most comfortable results from your visit.

Before Your Wax

A few simple steps beforehand make a real difference in comfort and results.

Do

  • Let hair grow to at least 1/4 inch — about the length of a grain of rice — for the wax to grip properly.
  • Gently exfoliate 24–48 hours before your appointment (not the same day) to help release trapped hairs.
  • Arrive with clean skin — a quick shower beforehand helps us give you the best result.
  • Wear loose, breathable clothing so nothing rubs on freshly waxed skin afterward.
  • Let your specialist know about any medications (like retinoids or Accutane), recent sun exposure, or if you're pregnant.
  • If you're sensitive to discomfort, an over-the-counter pain reliever about 30–45 minutes beforehand can help — check with your doctor if you're unsure.

Don't

  • Don't shave in between appointments — it disrupts the growth cycle we're working with.
  • Don't apply lotion, oil, or deodorant to the area on the day of your wax.
  • Don't tan or spend extended time in the sun right before your appointment — sensitive skin waxes less comfortably.
  • Don't overdo caffeine or alcohol beforehand, as both can heighten sensitivity.

After Your Wax

Your skin is a little more sensitive right after waxing — treat it gently for the next day or two.

Do

  • Keep the area clean and dry for the first 24 hours.
  • Wear loose, breathable clothing for at least 24–48 hours.
  • Apply a soothing aftercare product, like our Relax and Wax line, as recommended by your specialist.
  • Starting 2–3 days after your wax, gently exfoliate a couple of times a week to help prevent ingrown hairs.
  • Reach for Vanish PFB or Pure Deck Scrub if any ingrown hairs or bumps show up between visits.
  • Moisturize regularly between appointments to keep skin calm and smooth.

Don't

  • Avoid hot showers, baths, saunas, steam rooms, and swimming for 24–48 hours.
  • Avoid strenuous exercise or heavy sweating for 24 hours.
  • Avoid direct sun and tanning beds for 24–48 hours — freshly waxed skin burns more easily.
  • Avoid touching or picking at the skin, even if it's tempting.
  • Avoid perfumed lotions or deodorants on the treated area for 24 hours.
  • For Brazilian waxing, avoid sexual activity for 24 hours to let the skin fully settle.

Every body is different — if anything feels off after your appointment, just reach out and we'll help.
