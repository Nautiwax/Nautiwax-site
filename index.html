Origin Story

I am a mom, military spouse, and business owner. I build relationships with my clients and enjoy every opportunity to provide a true five-star service. At the studio I take tremendous pride in my work, and though I come from a speed-waxing background, I ventured out to establish a one-of-a-kind waxing studio that truly provides a more comfortable experience for my clients.

The name "Nautiwax" is a fun play on "Nautical." The studio provides services to men and women and brings the best of the tropical/coastal vibe into your experience. Thanks for considering our local small business for your waxing and hair removal needs!
